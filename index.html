About Me

A little bit about me

Hi, my name is Jonathan Geisler, and I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor's in Computer Science with a focus on Cybersecurity at Liberty University. I specialize in network security, penetration testing, and system hardening using tools like Nmap, Metasploit, Wireshark, and Docker across both Linux and Windows environments.

Recently, I’ve been competing in the NSA-sponsored NCAE Cyber Games, where I’ve worked hands-on defending against Red Team attacks and managing critical services such as FTP and SSH. Beyond tech, I also serve in student government, where I advocate for students and collaborate on campus-wide policy initiatives.

I’m passionate about securing systems and solving complex problems, and I’m eager to bring that drive and experience to a forward-thinking cybersecurity team.
